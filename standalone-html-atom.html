How it works:

  • Atoms vibrate around their equilibrium positions
  • Higher temperature = larger vibration amplitude
  • Atoms accumulate energy and can jump to adjacent vacancies
  • Vacancies shown as small dashed circles
  • Color shifts from blue (cold) to red (hot)
